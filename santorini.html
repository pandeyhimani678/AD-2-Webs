Santorini, Greece

Iconic sunsets, whitewashed villages, and Aegean serenity.

Overview

A romantic island known for its cliffside towns and sea views.

Santorini is a volcanic island famous for dramatic views, stunning sunsets from Oia, the Minoan ruins, and its volcanic-sand beaches. Wander through narrow alleyways, taste exceptional local wines, and watch the sky paint itself in golds and pinks.

Top Experiences

  • Sunset in Oia
  • Boat tour of the caldera
  • Wine tasting in local vineyards
  • Explore ancient Akrotiri ruins
